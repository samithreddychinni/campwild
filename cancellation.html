Cancellation Policy

Understanding our booking cancellation and refund policies

1. Overview

At CampWild, we understand that plans can change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to both our guests and our hosts. This policy outlines the terms and conditions for cancellations, modifications, and refunds for bookings made through our platform.

2. Standard Cancellation Policy

Our standard cancellation policy applies to most bookings unless otherwise specified on the listing. Refunds are determined by how far in advance you cancel.

Cancellation Timeframe Refund Amount
14+ days before check-in Full refund (100%)
7-13 days before check-in Partial refund (50%)
Less than 7 days before check-in No refund (0%)

Please note that the service fee is non-refundable unless you cancel within 48 hours of booking and at least 14 days before check-in.

3. Flexible Cancellation Policy

Some of our hosts offer a more flexible cancellation policy. Listings with this policy will be clearly marked.

Cancellation Timeframe Refund Amount
7+ days before check-in Full refund (100%)
3-6 days before check-in Partial refund (50%)
Less than 3 days before check-in No refund (0%)

4. Strict Cancellation Policy

Premium and high-demand properties may have a stricter cancellation policy. These listings will be clearly marked.

Cancellation Timeframe Refund Amount
30+ days before check-in Full refund (100%)
14-29 days before check-in Partial refund (50%)
Less than 14 days before check-in No refund (0%)

5. Extenuating Circumstances

In certain situations, you may be eligible for a refund under our Extenuating Circumstances Policy, even if you cancel after the refund deadline. Eligible circumstances include:

  • Unexpected serious illness or injury affecting you or a travel companion
  • Death of a family member or travel companion
  • Government-mandated obligations (jury duty, travel restrictions, etc.)
  • Severe natural disasters or severe weather incidents at the destination
  • Significant transportation disruptions that make it impossible to reach your destination

Documentation may be required to verify extenuating circumstances.

6. Cancellation Process

To cancel a reservation:

  1. Log in to your CampWild account
  2. Go to "My Trips" or "Bookings"
  3. Select the reservation you wish to cancel
  4. Click on "Cancel Reservation"
  5. Follow the prompts to complete the cancellation

You will receive an email confirmation once your cancellation has been processed.

7. Modifications to Bookings

If you need to modify your booking (change dates, number of guests, etc.), please contact us at least 7 days before your scheduled arrival. Modifications are subject to availability and may result in price adjustments.

8. No-Shows

If you do not show up for your reservation without prior cancellation, you will be charged the full amount of the booking with no refund.

9. Host Cancellations

In the rare event that a host needs to cancel a reservation, you will receive a full refund, including all fees. We will also assist you in finding alternative accommodations.

Need Help?

If you have any questions about our cancellation policy or need assistance with a cancellation, please don't hesitate to contact our customer support team.

What is CampWild?

CampWild is a platform that connects nature lovers with unique outdoor stays and experiences. We offer a wide range of camping options, from traditional tent camping to luxury glamping, as well as curated outdoor activities and adventures.

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is easy! Simply click on the "Sign In" button in the top right corner of the website, then select "Create an Account." You'll need to provide your name, email address, and create a password. You can also sign up using your Google or Facebook account for faster registration.

What destinations do you offer?

We currently offer camping destinations across various landscapes including mountains, forests, lakes, beaches, and deserts. Our properties are located throughout the United States, with plans to expand internationally in the near future. You can browse all available destinations on our Destinations page.

Is CampWild suitable for families with children?

Many of our destinations and experiences are family-friendly and offer activities suitable for children of various ages. Each listing includes information about family-friendliness and age recommendations for activities. We also offer family discounts on many of our bookings.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance for popular destinations, especially during peak season (summer months and holidays). Some of our premium experiences and glamping accommodations can be booked up to 6 months in advance. Last-minute bookings are sometimes available, but selection may be limited.

What is the minimum/maximum stay duration?

Most of our destinations have a minimum stay of 1-2 nights, though this may vary by location and season. There is typically no maximum stay duration, though stays longer than 14 days may require special arrangements. The specific minimum stay requirements are listed on each destination's booking page.

Can I modify my reservation after booking?

Yes, you can modify your reservation through your account dashboard. Changes to dates, number of guests, or adding experiences are subject to availability and may result in price adjustments. Modifications must be made at least 7 days before your scheduled arrival date. For assistance with modifications, please contact our customer support team.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and Apple Pay. For some destinations, we also offer payment plans through Affirm. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment system.

Is there a security deposit required?

Some of our destinations require a security deposit, particularly for glamping accommodations and premium experiences. The deposit amount varies by location and is clearly indicated during the booking process. Security deposits are fully refundable within 7 days after your stay, provided there is no damage to the property or equipment.

Are there any hidden fees?

We pride ourselves on transparency. All mandatory fees are included in the price displayed during booking. These may include cleaning fees, service fees, and applicable taxes. Optional add-ons such as equipment rentals, guided experiences, or meal packages are clearly marked as additional costs.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our standard cancellation policy offers a full refund if cancelled 14 days or more before the scheduled arrival date. Cancellations made 7-13 days before arrival receive a 50% refund. Cancellations less than 7 days before arrival are not eligible for a refund. Some destinations may have different policies, which will be clearly indicated on the booking page. For detailed information, please see our Cancellation Policy.

Do you offer trip insurance?

Yes, we offer optional trip insurance through our partner, TravelGuard. This insurance covers cancellations due to illness, injury, severe weather, and other unforeseen circumstances. The cost is typically 5-7% of your total booking and can be added during the checkout process.

What happens if the weather is bad during my stay?

We understand that outdoor activities are weather-dependent. If severe weather conditions make your stay unsafe, we will work with you to reschedule or provide a credit for future use. For minor weather disruptions, our hosts are prepared with alternative activities and accommodations. We recommend checking the weather forecast before your trip and packing accordingly.

What equipment is provided for camping?

The equipment provided varies by destination and accommodation type. For glamping and cabin stays, all necessary bedding, cooking equipment, and basic amenities are typically included. For traditional camping, you may need to bring your own tent and sleeping gear, or rent them for an additional fee. Each listing specifies exactly what is provided and what you need to bring.

Are guided activities included in the booking price?

Most guided activities and experiences are priced separately from accommodation bookings, allowing you to customize your stay. Some packages may include certain activities. You can browse and book experiences on our Experiences page or add them during the accommodation booking process.

Do I need prior experience for activities like hiking or fishing?

We offer activities for all skill levels, from beginner to advanced. Each experience listing includes information about the difficulty level and any prerequisites. For beginners, we provide instruction and all necessary equipment. For more advanced activities, our guides will ensure you have the skills needed before participation.

Still have questions?

If you couldn't find the answer you were looking for, our customer support team is here to help. Reach out to us via phone, email, or the contact form.

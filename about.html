About CampWild

Connecting nature lovers with unique outdoor experiences since 2015

Our Story

CampWild was founded in 2015 by a group of outdoor enthusiasts who were passionate about making nature more accessible to everyone. What started as a small platform connecting campers with private landowners has grown into a comprehensive service offering unique camping experiences across the country.

Our founders, Alex Rivera and Jamie Chen, met during a hiking trip in Yosemite and bonded over their shared frustration with the limited options for authentic outdoor experiences. They envisioned a platform that would not only connect people with beautiful camping destinations but also promote sustainable tourism and support local communities.

Today, CampWild partners with over 500 property owners nationwide, offering everything from basic tent camping to luxury glamping experiences. We remain committed to our core mission: helping people disconnect from their daily routines and reconnect with nature.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do at CampWild
